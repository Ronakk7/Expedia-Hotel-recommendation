About Our AI Hotel Recommendation System

How Our System Works

Our advanced AI system uses machine learning algorithms to analyze thousands of hotel features and provide personalized recommendations. The XGBoost model processes location scores, amenities, pricing history, and user preferences to predict the perfect hotel for each traveler.

Dataset & Training

We trained our model on over 100,000 hotel records from major European cities, including real booking data, guest reviews, and pricing trends. This comprehensive dataset ensures accurate predictions and reliable recommendations.

Prediction Accuracy

Our model achieves 95.3% accuracy in price predictions with an R² score of 0.94, making it one of the most reliable hotel recommendation systems available.
